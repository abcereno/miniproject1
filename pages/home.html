Features for effective and eficient inventory management

Real-Time Analytics

All the information gathered will be calculated and sent to the web app for analysis. The app will compare the performance of each product over time, such as daily, weekly, monthly, etc.

Notify

After the web app has analyzed the data, it will send notifications to the end-user, as per their pre-defined settings.

Alert System

When necessary, and if a stock runs low based on client's pre-defined settings, an alert will prompt on their page.

Inventory Tracking

Every item will be registered through barcode scanning and the information will be recorded in the web app.

Easy to Manage Product

Every time an item is taken out of the warehouse or inventory, the barcode will be entered into the order management section, where the information will be recorded and passed to the inventory tracking to deduct the item count.

Manage Staff Easily

The Inventory Management Web App will have individual logins for every person who is part of the company. Each person will have their own set of authorities and limitations.
